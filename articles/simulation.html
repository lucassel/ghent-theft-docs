👨‍💻 Simulation

One of the core foundations of Ghent Theft Auto is the ability to simulate or mock of real life processes happening in Ghent in the preset, as well as in the past and future.

Where we can't rely on historical data or when predicting into the future, we want to be able to rely on some kind of mocking of data, be it fully fake or driven by machine learning.

Simulation systems are big systems, coupling components into bigger processes.

Ghent Theft Auto draws inspiration from the concept of a digital twin, and comes with some very lofty goals.

  • Simulate the actual amount of humans inside the city at all times, every one of them with a full history and relations (260.000 entitites)
  • Simulate backwards and forwards along time

Life combines human and Time to give the appearance of an actual life inside the city.

TrafficSystem takes Open Street Map and builds a traffic system for people to move along.

The simulation combines Location and Profession in order to simulate the actual labour of citizens.

✨ History

Ghent Theft Auto has been my monster pet project since somewhere around 2013. The project originated as a GTA:IV mod but as my skills progressed, hardware evolved, data availability increased,.. it has since turned into an application that uses a more accessible game engine. In this case, Unity.

During my (failed) Architecture studies, I wanted to show one of my designs inside of a game engine.

I knew it was possible because there are tons of mods available for a vast array of games, every single on of them basically altering the game to the will of the mod creator.

Putting an architectural design inside of GTA:IV was cool and garnered some positive reactions for my classmates and teacher, but it wasn't until I started importing 3D models and textures that had a Ghent origin that people really started paying attention!
